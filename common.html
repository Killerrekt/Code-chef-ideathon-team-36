COMMON MEDICATIONS

Paracetamol

Paracetamol is used for pain relief and fever. It is used to relieve pain in conditions like headache, muscle pain, or dental pain. Paracetamol should be taken with food or milk to prevent upset stomach. The common side effect of Paracetamol are Nausea, Vomiting, Insomnia (difficulty in sleeping), Headache, Constipation, Itching. Also one should avoid consuming alcohol while taking this medicine as it can increase your risk of stomach problems.

Calpol

Calpol 500 tablet is used for the treatment of fever and pain including headache, muscle ache, tooth ache, periods pain and pain related to muscle and joints. The chances of getting side effects with Calpol are rare if taken in the recommended dose. In rare cases you may experience a skin rash, itching, blisters, allergic reaction and bruising.

Dolo 650

Dolo 650 tablet is used for treatment of fever, toothache, headache, migraine, backache, body pain, musculoskeletal pain. But one shouldn't take this medication if you already taking medicine that contains paracetamol. Chances of developing side effects with the Dolo 650 are rare if taken within the recommended doses. However, in rare cases, you may experience - Skin rash, Itching, Blisters, Allergic reaction
